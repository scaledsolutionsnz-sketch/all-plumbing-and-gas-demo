What we do

Expert plumbing
and gas, done right.

From a leaking tap to a full gas installation — Glenn handles it all. Every job is completed to a high standard with the experience and certification to back it up.

General Plumbing

Repairs, maintenance, and new installations for residential and commercial properties. Leaks, burst pipes, taps, toilets — sorted fast and done properly.

Gas Fitting

Certified gas fitting for LPG and natural gas systems. New installations, appliance connections, pressure testing, and compliance certification.

Hot Water Systems

Supply and installation of hot water cylinders, heat pump water heaters, and continuous flow (califont) systems. Replacements and upgrades handled end-to-end.

24/7 Emergency Callouts

Burst pipes, gas leaks, or a failed hot water cylinder — Glenn is available around the clock. Fast response, professional fix, any time of day or night.

Renovations & Fit-outs

Bathroom and kitchen renovations including all plumbing and gas work. New builds and renovations completed to code, on time, and on budget.

Handyman & Maintenance

General handyman work and property maintenance across Greymouth and the West Coast. A reliable trade professional for those jobs that need doing right.
